# Customer Journey

## 1. Discover
Customer visits the homepage and browses by occasion, category, or featured products.

## 2. Select
Customer chooses a physical product, personalized website, or a combo.

## 3. Personalize
Customer adds recipient name, occasion, message, photos, videos, date, handwritten note, and other supported fields.

## 4. Checkout
Customer completes guest checkout using name, mobile, and email.

## 5. Payment
Customer pays using UPI or eligible COD rules.

## 6. Fulfillment
Team prepares the physical gift, builds the personalized website, generates the dynamic QR, and prints the QR card.

## 7. Delivery
Physical product is dispatched to the recipient or customer.

## 8. Scan
Recipient scans the QR code and opens the personalized website.

## 9. Experience
Recipient sees messages, media, animations, theme styling, and memory content.

## 10. Follow-up
Customer can return using email or mobile to view order history and future purchases.
