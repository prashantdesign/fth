# Executive Summary

## Project Name
From The Heart (FTH)

## Overview
From The Heart is a premium gifting platform that combines physical gifts with personalized digital experiences. Customers can purchase gift hampers, personalized websites, and QR-powered gift experiences through a unified platform.

## Objectives
- Create memorable gifting experiences.
- Offer DIY and managed personalized websites.
- Generate dynamic QR codes linked to gift experiences.
- Support guest checkout with smart customer recognition.
- Provide a scalable PHP/MySQL platform compatible with Hostinger.

## Version 1 Features
- E-commerce
- Personalized Website Builder
- Dynamic QR Platform
- Inventory & BOM
- Shipping Engine
- Coupon Engine
- Admin Panel
- Reports & Analytics

## Future Versions
- Loyalty
- Referral
- Wallet
- AI Recommendations
- Multi-vendor Marketplace
- Mobile Applications
