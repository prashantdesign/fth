# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

# Purpose
Define the business goals, scope, stakeholders, and functional vision for the From The Heart platform.

## Business Vision
Create India's leading gifting platform that blends premium physical gifts with personalized digital experiences.

## Target Customers
- Individuals
- Couples
- Families
- Corporate clients
- Students
- NRIs

## Revenue Streams
- Gift Hampers
- Greeting Cards
- Personalized Websites
- QR Experiences
- Gift Wrapping
- Express Delivery
- Premium Customization

## Business Objectives
- Simplify gifting.
- Increase customer retention.
- Enable repeat gifting.
- Build a scalable platform.
- Expand globally.

## Success Metrics
- Orders per day
- Average order value
- Repeat purchase rate
- QR scans
- Website visits
- Conversion rate
- Customer satisfaction

## Stakeholders
- Founder
- Operations Team
- Design Team
- Developers
- Customers
- Delivery Partners
- Payment Gateway Providers

## Project Scope
Version 1 includes eCommerce, personalized websites, QR system, inventory, shipping, coupons, analytics, and admin management.

Version 2 will introduce AI, loyalty, referrals, wallet, marketplace, and mobile apps.
