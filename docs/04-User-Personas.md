# User Personas

## Persona 1: Gift Buyer
- Purchases gifts for birthdays, anniversaries, festivals, and special moments.
- Wants fast selection and easy checkout.

## Persona 2: Personalized Buyer
- Wants photos, names, wishes, and unique emotional presentation.
- Values customization and memorable experiences.

## Persona 3: Combo Buyer
- Buys physical gift + personalized website + QR card.
- Wants a premium surprise experience at an affordable price.

## Persona 4: Repeat Customer
- Returns for multiple occasions.
- Expects order history, quick reordering, and trust.

## Persona 5: Admin/Operations Team
- Manages products, orders, shipping, templates, QR codes, and reports.
- Needs a powerful dashboard and clear workflows.
