# Functional Requirements

## 1. Public Website
- Display home page with featured products and occasions.
- Provide category and occasion navigation.
- Support product search.
- Display product details, pricing, shipping info, and personalization options.

## 2. Guest Checkout
- Allow checkout without account creation.
- Collect name, mobile number, and email.
- Use email/mobile OTP for future order lookup.

## 3. Personalized Orders
- Allow recipient name, occasion, message, media uploads, delivery date, and handwritten note.
- Hide product price in gift-mode invoices.
- Support add-ons such as handwritten note and QR card.

## 4. Personalized Websites
- Offer DIY and managed creation modes.
- Provide 4-5 launch templates.
- Support theme editing, custom colors, music, password protection, QR generation, and guest wishes.

## 5. QR System
- Generate dynamic QR codes.
- Support downloads in PNG, SVG, and PDF.
- Track analytics and allow redirect changes.

## 6. Shipping
- Apply free shipping above ₹199.
- Apply ₹39 below threshold.
- Allow product-wise and location-wise overrides.

## 7. Payments
- Support UPI.
- Support COD only in selected locations.
- Allow admin-managed COD rules.

## 8. Inventory
- Track product stock.
- Track components for hamper BOM.
- Notify low stock.

## 9. Admin Panel
- Manage products, orders, templates, QR codes, shipping, coupons, inventory, reports, users, and settings.

## 10. Reports
- Show sales, order status, customer, QR, shipping, and inventory reports.
- Allow export to CSV or Excel.
